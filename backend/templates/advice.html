Pacing Advice

Design your week like a coach would.

Your commitments need rhythm. Use these prompts and generated ideas to protect energy, focus, and recovery.

Good pacing covers

  • Focus sprints for deep work.
  • Recovery breaks to reset your nervous system.
  • Connection blocks to keep relationships nourished.

Focus rituals

Anchor your most strategic work when your energy peaks.

  • Try a Tuesday morning makers block (90 minutes, no meetings).
  • Protect your first calendar block after lunch for creative thinking.

Recovery rituals

Micro-breaks help you sustain performance and mood.

  • Schedule a 10-minute stretch session after your longest call.
  • Use a breathing exercise before presenting or pitching.

Connection rituals

Relationship fuel keeps the mission meaningful.

  • Send one gratitude note mid-week to someone who helped you.
  • Book a shared break with a teammate to decompress.
